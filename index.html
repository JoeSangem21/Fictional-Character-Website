Harvey Specter

Harvey Specter was the main character from the TV Show SUITS. He was the main character in the show when it ran in 2011 to 2019 for 9 seasons.

Harvey Specter Famous Quotes

Harvey Specter lives in New York City and works as a big lawyer in Manhattan New York. He works for the fictional firm Pearson Hardman as a senior partner. In the firm his team is Mike Ross, Donna Paulsen, Louis Louis Litt, and Alex Williams. Mike Ross is Harvey's best friend and is really smart despite not even going to college and going to law school. Donna Paulsen is Harvey's secretary and wife. Louis Litt is Harvey's colleage in the law firm. Harvey and Louis have a friends-enemies relationship. Finally, Alex Williams is a rival, colleage and friend to Harvey Specter in the firm. Harvey Specter went to Harvard Law School and is very smart, rich, tough but hardworking.

Harvey Specter Favorite Moments

Harvey Specter lives in his luxurious apartment in NYC and was raised in Long Island, NY. He is married to his secretary Donna Paulson.
